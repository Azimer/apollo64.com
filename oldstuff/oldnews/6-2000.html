June 1st, 2000 [Phrodide] Status Update

I am glad to say the project has finally stablized itself, and we are now working on a few things:

  • A dynarec engine should be put in soon. Nothing out of the ordinary for this.
  • We've just had the memory module rewritten, and greatly simplified. A read from N64 memory takes 5 instructions and requires no look up table. Because of the memory simplification, we disabled controllers for now.
  • Our general priorities:
    • Exception Processing (Including TLB exceptions)
    • Dynarec
    • Support for 24 and 32 bit color modes
    • Audio (Interrupt faking at least)
    • RSP/RDP

    May 19th, 2000 [Azimer]

    A few bugs have been found that really should be avoided if possible:

  • Running a second rom image will not necessarily work and may slow down your machine. After using the image, close out the emulator completely and restart. The reason is not known yet.
  • There is no extension support for *.rom images. You must find them manually, this will be updated in the next release.
  • Do not attempt to pause the emulator. You will not be able to unpause it :)
  • Lastly, make sure you are in 16bit or 15bit color mode. If not, you will not be able to use the emulator.

    Thanks to Emul8or, LP-S, and Zersion for the tips.
